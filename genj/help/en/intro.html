GenJ is a viewer and editor for genealogic data and is written in 100% pure Java. A small team of people have contributed to this development and since it is released under GPL you're invited to download the source code and/or join the team.

This online help gives you a head start about GenJ's features and tells a little bit about its inner workings and hidden specials :). Having a powerful tool for working with genealogic information like GenJ is great but some things are not quite easy to find for the novice user. Please read the sections about Working with Views and Working With Context Menues very thoroughly because they cover the important basics of the main GenJ concepts.
