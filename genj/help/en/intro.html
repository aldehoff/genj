GenJ - Genealogy J

GenJ is a genealogy viewer and editor written in 100% pure Java. A small team of people have contributed to this development and since it is released under GPL you're invited to download the source code and/or join the team.

This online help gives you a head start about GenJ's features and tells a little bit about its inner workings and hidden specials :). Having a powerfull editor is nice because of all its capabilities but some things are not quite easy to find for the novice user. Please read the section about entities very thoroughly because it covers how to manage and enter your genealogic data.

By choosing one of the section on the left side you can jump right in to a specific topic.

Enjoy!
