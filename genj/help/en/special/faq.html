Q - What is GenJ ?
A - A viewer and editor for genealogic data stored in Gedcom format.

Q - Is there a mailing list and/or a newsgroup devoted to GenJ ?
A - There is no newsgroup, but two active mailing lists freely available by subscription :

  • genj-user@lists.sourceforge.net for open discussion, comments, questions about GenJ.
  • genj-developer@lists.sourceforge.net for development issues by interested people.
If you want to subscribe to one or both of those mailing lists, because you want to participate in discussion, ask questions regarding GenJ, help in development, just visit GenJ Homepage at SourceForge, and follow the links.

Q - How do I get pictures to show up in Tree View?
A - To show a picture for every person in the main Tree view add a multimedia property (OBJE) to your individuals. Simply bring up an indivdual in Edit View, choose the topmost property INDI, click on '+' and choose OBJE to be added. Point the sub-property OBJE|FILE to a file in your computer's file-system (click on the button 'choose file') and the images will be picked up by Tree View (it's looking for INDI:OBJE:FILE automatically).
