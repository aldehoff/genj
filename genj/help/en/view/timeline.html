Timeline View

Note: All views in GenJ work on the same underlying model that captures the genealogic data. You should have familiarized yourself with the general concept of Working with Views before drilling down to individuals views.

The timeline shows all events stored in a Gedcom file. An event is a special Gedcom-property like BIRTh, DEATh, RESIdence or GRADuation. This view puts all events on a scale starting before the first event and ending after the last event. The events are put into a layout from left to right (and top to bottom). Scroll around to inspect the events that happened around a specific time.

The toolbar at the lower/right offers additional functionality:

  1. You can choose a zoom-level by clicking on the zoom-widget (double-click restores 100%)
