Navigator View

GenJ offers several ways to navigate through your genealogical data. You can pick individuals and families in the Tree View, select entities in the Table View or browse properties in the Edit View and jump to other referenced entities. As a global navigating element the Navigator View allows you to follow relationships of individuals globally - other views will follow and show the individual you've navigated to.
